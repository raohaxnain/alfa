FAQ's

What is a transaction? add

We estimate your monthly fee based on transaction volume. For example, a supplier’s invoice to you which need to pay would be reflected as 2 transactions: 1 for the entering of bill, and 1 for producing a corresponding cheque/payment. If you are not sure, don’t worry. We can help you assess your transaction volume.

Do you offer desktop or cloud accounting services? add

We work with both, desktop and cloud-based accounting applications. We can carry-on with your existing applications or help migrating to more efficient solutions if needed.

How do you collect documents from your clients?add

For large paper volume, we arrange secured documents delivery to our office. For ongoing bookkeeping, you can provide us documents by email or web browser using desktop, laptops or smartphones.

Are there custom packages available?add

Yes, absolutely! The above mentioned price packages are for general guidelines. We understand that your business may have unique accounting needs so we are happy to offer you customized pricing that suits you the best.

My business activity fluctuates greatly. Are there hourly rates available?add

Yes, although most of our clients go with fixed monthly plans yet we can make hourly arrangements if this is something you prefer.
